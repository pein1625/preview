  3. History
2007 Starting mass production. Lege Vietnam obtained ISO9002 certificate.
2006 Established Vietnam branch in Bac Ninh.
2005 Decided to set up Vietnam branch in Bac Ninh Province. Due to convenience position, property right, production, sales and connection of customer.
2003 China Branch obtained ISO9002 certificate.
2002 China branch - Tien Din Metal Works Co., Ltd. established in June.
1997 Attained ISO9002 certificate (Taiwan).
1990 Introduced 5S activity, conducted training of entire-staff on quality concepts. stablished secondary process production line and assembly line for uplifting product stability.
1989 Moved into new plant, the present address in Sulin, Taipei, for designing and manufacturing die casting molds for OEM motorcycle part products.
1988 Changed company name into Lege-Tech Enterprise Co., Ltd. Developed 3D AD/CAM and CNC cutting machine center. For Designing and Manufacturing die casting molds for OEM car part products.
1981 Setup Molding Department for producing molds and zinc alloy parts.
1979 Moved to Hsinchuang, and change name to Lege-Tech Enterprise Limited.
1978 Changed company name into Lege-Tech Enterprise Co., Ltd. Developed 3D CAD/CAM and CNC cutting machine center. For Designing and Manufacturing die casting molds for OEM car part products.
