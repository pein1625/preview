About Us

Vietnam is currently an attractive destination to foreign investors for its geographic location, socio-political stability, fast economic growth and abundant human resources. Specially, Vietnam is increasingly integrating into the regional and the world economy, actively improving the legal system and investment environment.

However, the support for foreign investors in exploring local market, finding partners and locations, setting up and operating their projects in Vietnam is still limited, time-consuming and costly.

Grasping foreign investors’ need of business and investment connections and access to official information in analysing and making their investment decision, Vietnam Investment Promotion and Associates Corporation (IPAVIETNAM) was founded in 2012 by experienced and knowledgeable investment and economic experts, who had held senior leadership positions in the State agencies of trade and foreign direct investment, with the desire to be a trustworthy and effective partner of foreign investors in Vietnam.

IPAVIETNAM, organized and operated in the standard form of international Investment Promotion Agencies (IPAs), has close relationship with Government, ministries and agencies, local authorities, international organizations, business associations and promises to be a reliable companion to help foreign investors with their business and investment in Vietnam, from seeking business opportunities, finding investment locations and local partners, getting business licenses, setting up factories to operating production, doing business and solving difficulties arising.

Growing your business and investment in Vietnam effectively is our top target and mission!

Nguyen Dinh Nam l Founder & CEO
